Damage: 32–80 (varies by source)

Visual: Purple triple-layer circles (#8e44ad → #c39bd3 → #e8daef), soft purple glow

Sub-variants (applied on top):

Fast Void (isFastVoid)

More intense visual: pink/magenta layers (#b83dba → #e91e63 → #ff69b4) with a motion trail
Yellow warning glow pulses if teleport is imminent
Teleport Bullet (isTeleportBullet)

Travels normally, then teleports after a timer (20–60 frames depending on source)
Reappears near the player at speed 3–4.5
Hit damage capped at 45 by a safety clamp in collision code
Has a 30-frame cooldown window after hitting — only one teleport bullet can deal damage per 0.5 s
Final Attack Bullet (isFinalAttackBullet)

Same as teleport void bullet but with faster teleport speed (higher multiplier)
Regular+ Bullet (isRegularPlus)
Size: 8–10×8–10
Damage: 40–85 (varies by source)
Visual: Layered orange-red rectangle (#ff4444 → #ff8800 → #ffaa00) with orange glow; continuously emits 3 small flame particles per frame
Special effect on hit: inflicts health drain — 2 damage every ~0.33 seconds for 3 seconds (bypasses shield)
